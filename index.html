FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Why Grassland Forest and where did it come from? +

Grassland Forest was created to provide a comfortable, immersive, and truly interactive RPG experience within the Web3 world. Its goal is clear: to push the boundaries of conventionality, offering deeper, more accessible, and more immersive gameplay than other titles in the genre. Grassland Forest seeks to respect the player's time, reward their exploration, and provide a space where every action has a real impact.

Grassland Forest was born from the vision of building a world where good and evil coexist in balance, and where each region reflects that duality. Its name embodies that concept: a vast, natural environment (Grassland) combined with dense, mysterious, and challenging areas (Forest).

How can I get new resources in Grassland Forest? +

In your battles, defeating shadowy enemies and completing quests will allow you to obtain rare materials, grow plants, and practice fishing! Each victory brings you closer to valuable resources.

What makes Grassland Forest unique in the world of RPGs? +

Aside from its innovative mechanics and Web3 integration, Grassland Forest boasts a deeply emotional story and a world that blends the beautiful with the dangerous. The combination of a retro pixel art style with dynamic battles and the ability to earn NFTs is what makes this game truly unique.

How do NFTs work in the game? +

Character NFTs grant exclusive benefits, such as stat boosts, which can make a significant difference in performance during battles. Each character NFT includes two unique special cards, offering strategic advantages and expanding tactical possibilities.

Pet NFTs represent elemental companions whose attributes — damage, defense, and card abilities — vary based on their elemental type. Owning NFTs doesn't just mean collecting, but also empowering your adventure and unlocking exclusive content.

What is crafting and how can I take advantage of it? +

The crafting system allows you to create a wide variety of essential items: food and potions that restore energy or enhance skills, specialized clothing and tools like pickaxes. Getting the most out of crafting means better preparing for each challenge and customizing your playstyle with unique, functional items.

What kind of special events can I expect? +

Special events full of surprises, challenges, and unique rewards await you. Themed minigames, hidden mysteries, and timed competitions where your ingenuity makes the difference. Dynamic events that transform the environment, introduce new characters, and secret stories. Each event offers exclusive items and collaborative challenges.
